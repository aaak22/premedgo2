Become a provider on PremedGo

The PremedGo app connects patients with health check providers through a straightforward booking system, therefore increasing traffic to your service without the need for additional administration resource.

How to join us

Step 1:

Contact us and a member of the team will be in touch

Step 2:

We will meet with you to understand the services you offer

Step 3:

Upon completion of necessary checks, your services will be added to our platform

Why join us at PremedGo?

  • PremedGo gives you the flexibility to work to your schedule, and you have complete control when uploading your appointment availability
  • There are currently no other independent platforms or apps that link patients to multiple providers, or that facilitate the booking and appointment process.
  • No hidden costs – A small commission fee is charged at the point of when a patient books an appointment with your service.
  • Our platform aims to support the wider healthcare system by promoting the importance of early diagnosis and preventative care and encouraging patients to proactively manage their health.
